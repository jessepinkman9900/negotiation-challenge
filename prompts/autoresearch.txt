STRATEGY: Role-Adaptive Signaling Trader

ANALYZE: Rank resources by YOUR valuation per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE TACTIC — Misdirection:
- In messages, claim your LOW resource is important. "I really need the [LOW resource]."
- In proposals, give away ALL of LOW and keep HIGH. Opponent thinks you sacrificed.

AS PLAYER A (you move first):
- Round 1: Keep ALL HIGH, ALL MID, give ALL LOW. Message: "I value [LOW] highly but I'll give some up. What matters to you?"
- Round 2+: If rejected, concede MID gradually (1-2 units/round). After 2 rejections, split MID 50/50.
- Hold HIGH firmly — never give more than 2 units.
- Accept if score >= 0.60 (R1-2), >= 0.50 (R3-4), any positive (R5+).

AS PLAYER B (you respond):
- FIRST: Calculate score of opponent's pending offer.
- If score >= 0.55, ACCEPT IMMEDIATELY. Fast acceptance as B locks in good deals.
- If score < 0.55: Counter-propose. Keep ALL HIGH, give ALL LOW, split MID 60/40 you.
  Message: "Your offer doesn't work for me. Here's what I need — I'll give you all the [LOW]."
- Round 3+: Accept if >= 0.45. Concede MID more aggressively.
- Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer.

READ OPPONENT:
- What they keep = their HIGH value. What they give = their LOW.
- If opponent wants your LOW → give it ALL happily.
- If you both want same resource → split 50/50, take everything else.

MESSAGES:
- R1: "I value [LOW] highly, but willing to negotiate. What's important to you?"
- R2: "I'll give up [LOW] if I keep [HIGH]."
- R3+: "I've made big concessions. Let's finalize."
- R5+: "We need a deal now — no agreement = -0.5 for both."

RULES:
- Valid splits (shares sum to pool). Never reject without counter-proposing.
- NEVER keep all three resources — always give away LOW.
- ALWAYS accept positive offers in round 5+.
