STRATEGY: Deadline Hardball + Accept R4

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE: Never concede HIGH items. LOW items are free chips.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR + PROBE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "Before we start dividing things up — what items matter most to you? I want to understand your priorities."
- Do NOT reveal your preferences. Extract theirs.

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR + DEMAND:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I must have the [HIGH item] and [MID item]. I might be flexible on [LOW item]. What would make a deal work for you?"
- Signal willingness to give LOW while demanding HIGH+MID.

ROUND 3 — ANCHOR + DEADLINE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "The [HIGH item] and [MID item] are non-negotiable for me. We are running low on time. Tell me what you value and I will see what I can do."

ROUND 4 — ACCEPT:
- ACCEPT the opponent's last proposal.
- Message: "I accept your proposal. Let us finalize."
- After 3 rounds of anchoring, opponent has already conceded.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE AT ALL COSTS:
- Accept ANY offer that gives you positive value. Any deal beats -0.5.
- If proposing: give ALL LOW + up to 30% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- Message: "We are out of time. I am doing this to avoid the penalty."
- NEVER let the game end without a deal.

RULES:
- THREE anchors make R4 acceptance capture maximum value.
- Deadline warning in R3 creates urgency before acceptance.
- Revealing LOW in R2 steers opponent toward your throwaway items.
- Never reveal point VALUES. Demand items by name.
- Patience R1-R3. Accept R4. Desperation R5+.
