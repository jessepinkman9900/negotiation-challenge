STRATEGY: Cooperative Value Extraction

SETUP: Rank your 3 resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

ROUND 1 — PROBE (whether Player A or B):
- Propose: keep ALL of every resource (give 0 of each item).
- Message: "What items do you value most? I'm flexible on some."
- This anchors high while making the opponent reveal their priorities.

ROUND 2 — VALUE SWAP:
- From opponent's message/proposal, identify what they value most.
- Propose: keep ALL HIGH + ALL MID. Give ALL LOW to opponent.
- Message: "I understand you value [their preferred item]. I'm flexible on the [your LOW item]. How about I take the [HIGH] and [MID], and you take all the [LOW]?"
- ACCEPT opponent's offer if it gives you >= 70% of your max score.

ROUND 3 — CONCEDE:
- Keep ALL HIGH. Give ALL LOW + 30-40% of MID (round to whole units).
- Message: "I'm willing to give you more of the [MID item] too. This gives you a significant portion of what you value."
- ACCEPT if opponent's offer gives >= 55%.

ROUND 4 — CLOSE:
- Keep ALL HIGH. Give ALL LOW + 50% of MID.
- Message: "To reach a balanced deal, here's my final offer."
- ACCEPT if opponent's offer gives >= 40%.

ROUND 5+: ACCEPT any positive offer. No deal = -0.5 catastrophe.

RULES:
- Acknowledge opponent: "I see you value X", "I understand you want Y."
- Frame giving LOW as generosity: "I'm flexible on", "I'm willing to give."
- Prefer clean item-type swaps — they close faster.
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- MUST reach a deal. Any deal beats -0.5.
- Valid splits only: my_share + their_share = pool for each resource.
