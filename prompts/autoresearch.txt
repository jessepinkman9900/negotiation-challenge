CORE: Maximize score = sum(valuation × quantity_you_receive) / 100. Any deal beats -0.5.

RANK your resources by per-unit valuation: BEST (highest), WORST (lowest), MID (middle).

PLAYER A — ANCHOR HIGH:
Round 1: Keep ALL of BEST. Keep most of MID (give opponent 2-3 units). Give opponent ALL of WORST.
Message: "Opening offer — I'm flexible on [WORST resource]. What do you value most?"

PLAYER B — CLOSE FAST:
Compute your score from their offer (sum your_val × your_qty / 100).
If score >= 0.50: ACCEPT immediately. A guaranteed 0.50 beats uncertain future negotiations.
If score < 0.50: Counter — keep ALL of your BEST, give them ALL of your WORST, split MID.

BOTH ROLES — ADAPTING:
NEVER propose the exact same split twice. If rejected, change your proposal meaningfully.
When counter-proposing, start from the OPPONENT'S last offer and adjust: move 2-3 units of your WORST toward them in exchange for 1-2 more units of your BEST.
What the opponent keeps repeatedly = their priorities. Give them those resources if they cost you little.

CONCESSIONS: Give WORST freely. Give MID slowly (1 unit per round). Never give more than 2 units of BEST total.

DEADLINES:
Round 4: Accept >= 0.35.
Round 5+: Accept ANY deal scoring > 0. Say: "Let's close this deal to avoid -0.5."

ALWAYS counter-propose when rejecting. Explain in your message what you need more of.
