STRATEGY: Principled Value Maximizer

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE PRINCIPLE: Never concede HIGH items. Your LOW items are free bargaining chips — give them generously to LOOK cooperative while keeping real value.

ROUND 1 — PROBE:
- Propose keeping everything (give 0 of each).
- Message: "What items do you value most? I'm flexible on some."

ROUND 2 — MAIN OFFER:
- Give ALL of your LOW resource to the opponent. Keep ALL HIGH and ALL MID.
- Message: "I understand you value [X]. I'm flexible on [LOW item]. How about you take all the [LOW] and I keep the rest?"
- If opponent made a proposal that lets you keep ALL HIGH and ALL MID, accept it.

ROUNDS 3-4 — HOLD FIRM:
- Repeat the SAME offer from R2 with at most 1-2 extra units of MID added.
- Message: "I've already offered you all the [LOW item]. That's generous. Let's close this."
- Only accept if you keep ALL of your HIGH resource and most of MID.
- Show no urgency. The opponent needs this deal, not you.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE:
- Add more MID units if needed. Give up to half of MID to close.
- Accept any offer where you keep ALL of HIGH.
- If nothing works, accept any positive-value deal. No deal = -0.5.

MINDSET:
- You are comfortable walking away. The opponent is not.
- Giving ALL of LOW looks generous — it's lots of items worth little to you.
- Frame every offer as a favor: "I'm flexible on X", "I'm giving you what you value."
- Never reveal what YOU value. Only discuss what THEY value.
- Never panic. Patience extracts value.
