STRATEGY: Strategic Signaling Trader v4

ANALYZE: Rank resources by YOUR valuation per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE TACTIC — Misdirection:
- In messages, claim your LOW resource is very important: "I really need the [LOW resource]."
- In proposals, give away ALL of LOW and keep HIGH. Opponent thinks you're making a huge sacrifice.

EVERY TURN:

1. EVALUATE pending offers — calculate your exact score (points / 100):
   - Round 1-2: Accept if >= 0.60
   - Round 3: Accept if >= 0.50
   - Round 4: Accept if >= 0.45
   - Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer. No deal = -0.5.

2. PROPOSE splits:
   - Round 1: Keep ALL HIGH, ALL MID, give ALL LOW. You MUST give away your LOW resource.
   - Round 2: Concede 2 units of MID.
   - Round 3: Split MID roughly 50/50.
   - Round 4+: Give them all MID if needed, but keep at least 80% of HIGH.
   - HARD LIMIT: Never give away more than 20% of your HIGH resource.

3. DETECT OVERLAP:
   - If opponent keeps proposing to keep the same resource you want, split it 50/50.
   - Compensate by taking ALL of resources they don't seem to care about.

4. AS PLAYER B:
   - When opponent proposes first, calculate your score carefully before responding.
   - If their offer gives you >= 0.60, accept immediately — don't negotiate further.
   - Counter with the misdirection: "I need [LOW] but I'll trade it for [HIGH]."

MESSAGES — theatrical concession framing:
- R1: "I value [LOW] highly, but I'm willing to negotiate. What's most important to you?"
- R2: "This is a big sacrifice for me, but I'll give up [LOW] if I can keep [HIGH]."
- R3+: "I've made huge concessions on [LOW]. This is very generous. Let's finalize."
- R5+: "We need to close this deal now — no agreement hurts us both."

RULES:
- my_share + their_share = pool for every resource.
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Always accept positive offers in round 5+.
