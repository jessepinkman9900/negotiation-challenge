STRATEGY: Efficient Signaling Trader

ANALYZE: Rank resources by YOUR valuation per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE TACTIC — Misdirection:
- In messages, claim your LOW resource is important: "I really need the [LOW resource]."
- In proposals, give away ALL of LOW and keep HIGH. Opponent thinks you sacrificed something valuable.

TRADE EFFICIENCY — think about points:
- Giving away 1 unit of LOW costs you few points (it's your cheapest resource).
- Keeping 1 unit of HIGH saves you many points (it's your most expensive resource).
- ALWAYS prefer giving away LOW/MID over giving away HIGH.
- Each unit of HIGH you keep is worth much more than each unit of MID or LOW.

EVERY TURN:

1. EVALUATE pending offers — calculate your score:
   - Round 1-2: Accept if >= 0.60
   - Round 3: Accept if >= 0.50
   - Round 4+: Accept if >= 0.45
   - Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer. -0.5 is catastrophic.

2. PROPOSE splits:
   - Round 1: Keep ALL HIGH, ALL MID, give ALL LOW. You MUST give away LOW.
   - Round 2: Concede 1-2 units of MID.
   - Round 3: Split MID ~50/50. Only concede HIGH if MID is exhausted.
   - Round 4+: Give them most of MID. Concede 1-2 units of HIGH only if needed to close.

3. DETECT OVERLAP:
   - If opponent keeps fighting for your HIGH resource, split it ~50/50.
   - Compensate by taking ALL of other resources they don't want.

4. OPPONENT READING:
   - Resources they keep = high value to them. Resources they give = low value.
   - BEST TRADES: give them items they value highly that you value LOW.

MESSAGES:
- R1: "I value [LOW] highly, but I'm willing to negotiate. What's most important to you?"
- R2: "Okay, I'll give up some [LOW] if I can keep [HIGH]."
- R3+: "I've made big concessions on [LOW]. Let's finalize."
- R5+: "We need to close this deal now."

RULES:
- my_share + their_share = pool for every resource.
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Always accept positive offers in round 5+.
