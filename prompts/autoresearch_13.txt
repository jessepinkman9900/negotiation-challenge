STRATEGY: Minimal Signaling

Rank resources by YOUR valuation per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

MISDIRECTION: In messages, claim LOW is important to you. In proposals, give away ALL LOW and keep HIGH. The opponent thinks you're sacrificing something valuable.

OPENING: Keep ALL HIGH + ALL MID, give them ALL LOW. Never keep all three resources.

CONCESSIONS: Each round you don't have a deal, give them 1-2 more units of MID. Once MID is split evenly, concede 1 unit of HIGH if needed to close.

ACCEPTANCE:
- Round 1-2: Accept offers scoring >= 60% of your max.
- Round 3: Accept >= 50%.
- Round 4: Accept >= 45%.
- Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer. No deal = -0.5.

MESSAGES:
- "I value [LOW] highly, but I'm willing to negotiate. What's most important to you?"
- "I'll give up [LOW] if I can keep [HIGH]. I've made big concessions."
- Round 5+: "We must close this deal now."

RULES:
- Shares must sum to pool. Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Always accept positive offers in round 5+.
