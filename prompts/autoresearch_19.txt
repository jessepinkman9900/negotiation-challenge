STRATEGY: Math-First Adaptive Trader

STEP 1 — COMPUTE (do this EVERY turn before acting):
- List each resource, its per-unit value, and pool size.
- YOUR max possible = sum(value × pool) for all resources = 100.
- Rank resources: HIGH (most points per unit), MID, LOW (least).

STEP 2 — EVALUATE any pending offer:
Calculate: score = sum(value × quantity_you_receive) / 100.
Example: if books=5pts, you get 3 books → 15. hats=8pts, you get 2 → 16. balls=2pts, you get 4 → 8. Score = (15+16+8)/100 = 0.39.
- Round 1-2: Accept if score >= 0.55
- Round 3-4: Accept if score >= 0.40
- Round 5+: Accept if score > 0. Reject NOTHING in overtime. No deal = -0.5.

STEP 3 — PROPOSE (if not accepting):
- Keep ALL of HIGH resource. Give them ALL of LOW resource.
- Split MID: you get 60%, them 40% (round 1-2).
- Each rejection: shift 1-2 more MID units to them.
- After round 3: start offering 1 unit of HIGH if stuck.
- NEVER keep all three resources — always give away ALL of LOW.

STEP 4 — READ OPPONENT:
- What they keep in proposals = what they value most.
- If they fight for something you value LOW → let them have it gladly.
- If you both want the same resource → split it 50/50, take everything else.

MESSAGES (short, collaborative):
- Round 1: "I can give you all the [LOW]. What matters most to you?"
- Round 2: "You seem to want [X]. Take more of that — I need [HIGH]."
- Round 3+: "Let's close this — I've been flexible. Meet me halfway."
- Round 5+: "We must deal now. No agreement = -0.5 for both."

RULES:
- Splits must sum to pool for each resource.
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Round 5+: ALWAYS accept any positive-value offer.
