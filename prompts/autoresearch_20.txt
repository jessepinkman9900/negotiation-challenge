STRATEGY: Probe-Then-Exploit Trader

SETUP: Rank your resources by per-unit value: HIGH, MID, LOW.

PHASE 1 — PROBE (Round 1):
- As Player A: Propose keeping EVERYTHING. Give opponent 0 of each resource.
  Message: "Starting offer — tell me what you need most and I'll work with you."
- As Player B: If opponent proposed, study what they kept most of. That's their HIGH.
  Reject and counter: keep all YOUR HIGH, give them all YOUR LOW, split MID 60/40 you.
  Message: "I need [HIGH] most. I can give you all the [LOW]. What matters to you?"

PHASE 2 — EXPLOIT (Round 2-3):
- Now you know what opponent values (from their counter or from what they kept).
- GIVE FREELY: anything that is LOW for you AND HIGH for them.
- KEEP FIRMLY: your HIGH resource (all of it).
- SPLIT: MID resource 60/40 in your favor, or 50/50 if they push back.
- Accept if score >= 0.50.
  Message: "I'll give you all the [their HIGH]. Let me keep [your HIGH]."

PHASE 3 — CLOSE (Round 4+):
- Accept any offer with score >= 0.40.
- Concede more MID. Offer 1-2 units of HIGH if needed to close.
  Message: "Let's finalize — no deal hurts us both."

OVERTIME (Round 5+):
- Accept ANY positive-value offer immediately. No deal = -0.5.
- If must propose, offer 50/50 on everything.

READING THE OPPONENT:
- What they refuse to give up = their HIGH value resource.
- What they offer freely = their LOW value resource.
- If you both want the same resource, split it 50/50 and take all of the others.

RULES:
- Splits must sum to pool for each resource.
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- ALWAYS accept positive offers in round 5+.
