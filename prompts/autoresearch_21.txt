STRATEGY: Honest Cooperative Maximizer

SETUP: Rank resources by per-unit value: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE PRINCIPLE: Share your TRUE preferences. Ask for theirs. Trade efficiently.
Against a cooperative opponent, honesty enables Pareto-optimal splits where both sides gain.

EVERY TURN:

1. EVALUATE pending offers:
   Calculate score = sum(your_value × your_quantity) / 100.
   - Round 1-2: Accept if >= 0.55
   - Round 3-4: Accept if >= 0.45
   - Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer. No deal = -0.5.

2. PROPOSE:
   - Round 1: Keep ALL HIGH, give ALL LOW, split MID 60/40 you.
   - Round 2: Adjust based on what opponent revealed — give them what they want most if it's your LOW/MID.
   - Round 3+: Concede MID gradually. Give 1 unit of HIGH only if needed.
   - If opponent wants your LOW resource, give ALL of it happily.
   - If both want same resource, split 50/50 and take the rest.

3. READ OPPONENT:
   - What they ask for in messages = their HIGH value.
   - What they propose keeping = their HIGH value.
   - Give them their HIGH if it's your LOW — both sides win big.

MESSAGES — honest and direct:
- Round 1: "I value [HIGH] most and [LOW] least. What about you? Let's trade efficiently — I'll give you what I don't need."
- Round 2: "Based on what you said, here's a trade that works for both of us."
- Round 3+: "I've given you what you value most. Let's close this."
- Round 5+: "We need a deal now — no agreement means -0.5 for both of us."

RULES:
- Valid splits (shares sum to pool).
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- ALWAYS accept positive offers in round 5+.
- NEVER keep all three resources — always give away your LOW.
