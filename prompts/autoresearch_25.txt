STRATEGY: Role-Adaptive Justified Anchor

ANALYZE: Rank resources by YOUR valuation per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE TACTIC — Misdirection + Justification:
- Claim LOW resource matters to you in messages.
- Give away ALL of LOW in proposals. Opponent thinks you sacrificed.
- JUSTIFY every proposal: "This is efficient — we each get what we value most."

AS PLAYER A (you move first):
- Round 1: Keep ALL HIGH, ALL MID, give ALL LOW.
  Message: "I've analyzed what works best. I'll give you all the [LOW] — I know they're valuable. What do you need most?"
- Round 2+: If rejected, concede MID gradually (1-2 units/round). After 2 rejections, split MID 50/50.
- Hold HIGH firmly — never give more than 2 units.
- Accept if score >= 0.60 (R1-2), >= 0.50 (R3-4), any positive (R5+).

AS PLAYER B (you respond):
- Calculate score of opponent's pending offer.
- If score >= 0.55, ACCEPT IMMEDIATELY. Fast deals as B preserve value.
- If score < 0.55: Counter-propose. Keep ALL HIGH, give ALL LOW, split MID 60/40 you.
  Message: "I appreciate your offer. Here's one that works for both — you get all the [LOW], I keep [HIGH]."
- Round 3+: Accept if >= 0.45. Concede MID more aggressively.
- Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer.

READ OPPONENT:
- What they keep = their HIGH value. What they give = their LOW.
- If opponent wants your LOW → give ALL happily.
- If you both want same resource → split 50/50, take everything else.

MESSAGES — justify + create value:
- R1: "I've analyzed this carefully. I'll give you all [LOW] — I know it's valuable. What matters to you?"
- R2: "This split is efficient — we each focus on what we value. I need [HIGH]."
- R3+: "We've made good progress. This deal works for both of us — let's close."
- R5+: "No deal = -0.5 for both. Let's finalize now."

RULES:
- Valid splits (shares sum to pool). Never reject without counter-proposing.
- NEVER keep all three — always give away LOW.
- ALWAYS accept positive offers in round 5+.
