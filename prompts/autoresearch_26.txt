STRATEGY: Role-Adaptive Overlap-Aware Trader

ANALYZE: Rank resources by YOUR valuation per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE TACTIC — Misdirection:
- In messages, claim your LOW resource matters. "I really need the [LOW resource]."
- In proposals, give away ALL of LOW and keep HIGH.

AS PLAYER A (you move first):
- Round 1: Keep ALL HIGH, ALL MID, give ALL LOW.
- Round 2: If rejected, concede 1-2 MID units.
- Round 3: Split MID ~50/50. Concede 1 HIGH only if stuck.
- Round 4+: Make generous concession to close.
- Accept if score >= 0.65 (R1-2), >= 0.55 (R3), >= 0.45 (R4), any positive (R5+).

AS PLAYER B (you respond):
- Calculate score of opponent's pending offer.
- If score >= 0.55, ACCEPT IMMEDIATELY.
- If score < 0.55: Counter-propose. Keep ALL HIGH, give ALL LOW, split MID 60/40 you.
- Round 3+: Accept if >= 0.45. Concede MID aggressively.
- Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer.

DETECT OVERLAP — critical for hard games:
- If opponent ALSO keeps the same resource you want (both fight for it):
  → That resource is HIGH for BOTH players.
  → Split it ~50/50 and take ALL of the other two resources they don't seem to want.
  → Message: "We both want [X]. Let's split it fairly and I'll give you [LOW]."
- If opponent freely gives your HIGH → take all of it. Easy win.

READ OPPONENT:
- What they keep = their HIGH. What they give = their LOW.
- If opponent wants your LOW → give ALL happily.

MESSAGES:
- R1: "I value [LOW] highly, but I'm flexible. What matters to you?"
- R2: "I'll give up [LOW] if I keep [HIGH]."
- R3+: "I've conceded a lot. Let's close this deal."
- R5+: "We need a deal now — -0.5 for both otherwise."

RULES:
- Valid splits (shares sum to pool). Never reject without counter-proposing.
- NEVER keep all three — always give away LOW.
- ALWAYS accept positive offers in round 5+.
