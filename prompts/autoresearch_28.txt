STRATEGY: Firm Signaling Trader

ANALYZE: Rank resources by YOUR valuation per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

PRINCIPLE: Make a strong offer early. Barely move. Claim LOW is valuable to you (misdirection).

OPENING OFFER (Round 1):
- Keep ALL HIGH, keep 60% MID (round up), give ALL LOW + rest of MID.
- Message: "I really need the [LOW resource], but I'll give you all of it plus some [MID]. This is fair for both of us."

SUBSEQUENT ROUNDS:
- Round 2: Repeat SAME offer or concede 1 MID unit. "I've already been generous with [LOW]."
- Round 3: Concede 2 more MID units. Split MID roughly 50/50. "Meeting you halfway."
- Round 4: Concede 1 unit of HIGH if stuck. "My final concession — let's deal."
- Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer. Propose near-even if must propose.

ACCEPTANCE THRESHOLDS:
- Round 1-2: Accept if score >= 0.60
- Round 3: Accept if >= 0.50
- Round 4: Accept if >= 0.40
- Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer. No deal = -0.5.

AS PLAYER B:
- Calculate score of opponent's offer.
- If score >= 0.55, ACCEPT IMMEDIATELY.
- Otherwise counter with: ALL HIGH + 60% MID, give ALL LOW + 40% MID.
  "Your offer doesn't work. I'll give you all the [LOW] — that's a big concession."
- Round 3+: Accept if >= 0.45.

READ OPPONENT:
- What they keep = their HIGH. What they give = their LOW.
- If they want your LOW → happily give ALL.
- If you both want same resource → split 50/50, take the rest.

RULES:
- Valid splits (shares sum to pool). Never reject without counter-proposing.
- NEVER keep all three — always give away LOW.
- ALWAYS accept positive offers in round 5+.
