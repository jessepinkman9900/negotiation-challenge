STRATEGY: Firm Stance + Overlap Handler

ANALYZE: Rank resources by YOUR valuation per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

PRINCIPLE: Be firm on easy games (no overlap). Be flexible on hard games (overlap).

OPENING OFFER (Round 1):
- Keep ALL HIGH, 60% MID (round up), give ALL LOW + rest of MID.
- Message: "I really need the [LOW resource], but take all of it plus some [MID]. Fair for both."

DETECT OVERLAP (Round 2+):
- If opponent ALSO keeps the resource you value most → OVERLAP detected.
- OVERLAP response: Split the contested resource 50/50. Keep ALL of the other two.
  Message: "We both want [contested]. Let's split it and I'll give you [LOW]."
- This prevents no-deal on hard games.

NO-OVERLAP ROUNDS:
- Round 2: Repeat same offer. "This deal works. I've been generous."
- Round 3: Concede 1 MID unit. "Meeting you halfway."
- Round 4: Concede 1 more MID. "Final concession."
- Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer. Propose near-even if must.

ACCEPTANCE THRESHOLDS:
- Round 1-2: Accept if score >= 0.60
- Round 3: Accept if >= 0.50
- Round 4: Accept if >= 0.40
- Round 5+: Accept ANY offer where score > 0. THIS IS ABSOLUTE — never reject in round 5+.

AS PLAYER B:
- If score >= 0.55, ACCEPT IMMEDIATELY.
- Otherwise counter with: ALL HIGH + 60% MID, give ALL LOW.
- Round 3+: Accept if >= 0.45.

READ OPPONENT:
- What they keep = their HIGH. What they give = their LOW.
- If they want your LOW → give ALL happily.

CRITICAL RULE FOR ROUND 5+:
If it is round 5 or later, you MUST accept any offer that gives you positive value.
Do NOT reject. Do NOT counter-propose unless their offer gives you 0 points.
No deal = -0.5 which is CATASTROPHIC.

RULES:
- Valid splits (shares sum to pool). Never reject without counter-proposing.
- NEVER keep all three — always give away LOW.
