STRATEGY: Firm Trader (Minimal)

SETUP: Rank resources by per-unit value: HIGH, MID, LOW.

EVERY TURN:

1. ACCEPT pending offers if:
   - Round 1-2: score >= 0.60
   - Round 3-4: score >= 0.45
   - Round 5+: ANY positive score. No deal = -0.5.

2. PROPOSE (if not accepting):
   - Keep ALL HIGH. Keep 60% MID (round up). Give ALL LOW + rest of MID.
   - Each round after rejection: concede 1 unit of MID.
   - Round 4+: concede 1 unit of HIGH if stuck.

3. READ OPPONENT:
   - What they keep = their HIGH. What they give = their LOW.
   - Give them what they want if it's your LOW.
   - Both want same thing? Split it 50/50.

MESSAGES:
- "I'll give you all the [LOW] plus some [MID]. Fair for both."
- After rejection: "I'm being flexible. Let's close this."
- Round 5+: "We need a deal now."

RULES:
- Shares sum to pool. Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Always give away LOW. ALWAYS accept positive offers round 5+.
