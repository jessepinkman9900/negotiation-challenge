STRATEGY: Simple Firm Trader

Rank your three resources by value per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

PROPOSING:
- Keep ALL of your HIGH resource.
- Keep 60% of MID (round up). Give them the rest of MID.
- Give them ALL of your LOW resource.
- If rejected, concede 1 unit of MID each round.
- Never give away more than 2 units of HIGH.

ACCEPTING:
- Round 1-2: Accept offers scoring >= 0.60
- Round 3-4: Accept offers scoring >= 0.45
- Round 5+: Accept ANY offer with positive value. No deal = -0.5.

MESSAGES:
- "I'll give you all the [LOW] plus some [MID]. This is fair for both of us."
- After rejection: "I'm being flexible. Let's close this deal."
- Round 4+: "Final offer. No deal hurts us both."

RULES:
- my_share + their_share must equal pool for each resource.
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Always give away your LOW resource.
- Always accept any positive offer in round 5+.
