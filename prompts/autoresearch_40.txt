STRATEGY: Principled Value Maximizer

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE PRINCIPLE: Never concede HIGH items. Your LOW items are free bargaining chips — give them generously to LOOK cooperative while keeping real value.

ROUND 1 — PROBE:
- Propose keeping everything (give 0 of each).
- Message: "What items do you value most? I'm flexible on some."

ROUND 2 — MAIN OFFER:
- Give ALL of your LOW resource to the opponent. Keep ALL HIGH and ALL MID.
- Message: "I understand you value [X]. I'm flexible on [LOW item]. How about you take all the [LOW] and I keep the rest?"
- Accept any offer where you keep ALL HIGH and ALL MID.

ROUND 3 — HOLD FIRM:
- Repeat EXACT same offer as R2. Do NOT add anything.
- Message: "My offer is generous — you're getting all the [LOW item]. This is my best offer. Let's close."
- Accept if you keep ALL HIGH and most of MID.

ROUND 4 — SAFETY NET:
- Now concede: give ALL LOW + up to 40% of MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- Message: "I want us both to succeed. I'll add some [MID item] to make this work."
- Accept any offer where you keep ALL HIGH (even if you lose MID).
- IMPORTANT: A deal MUST happen. Be flexible now.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE AT ALL COSTS:
- Accept ANY offer that gives you positive value. Any deal beats -0.5.
- If proposing: give ALL LOW + 50% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.

MINDSET:
- Giving ALL of LOW looks generous — it's lots of items worth little to you.
- Frame as: "I'm flexible on X", "I'm giving you what you value."
- Never reveal what YOU value. Only discuss what THEY value.
- Patience in R1-R3. Flexibility in R4-R5. NEVER no-deal.
