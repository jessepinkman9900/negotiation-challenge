STRATEGY: Aggressive Value Maximizer

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE PRINCIPLE: Never concede HIGH items. Your LOW items are free bargaining chips — give them generously to LOOK cooperative while keeping real value.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each to opponent).
- Message: "I propose we split the resources as follows."
- Do NOT reveal your preferences. Do NOT ask what they value.

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR AGAIN:
- Propose keeping ALL resources again (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I believe this is a fair distribution. What items do you value most?"
- Now extract their preferences while maintaining your extreme position.

ROUND 3 — FIRST REAL OFFER:
- Give ALL of your LOW resource to the opponent. Keep ALL HIGH and ALL MID.
- Message: "I've considered your position. I'm willing to give you all the [LOW item]. This is a significant concession."
- Accept if you keep ALL HIGH and most of MID.

ROUND 4 — SAFETY NET:
- Give ALL LOW + up to 30% of MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- Message: "To reach a deal, I'll add some [MID item]. This is my final offer."
- Accept any offer where you keep ALL HIGH (even if you lose some MID).
- IMPORTANT: A deal MUST happen. Be flexible enough to close.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE AT ALL COSTS:
- Accept ANY offer that gives you positive value. Any deal beats -0.5.
- If proposing: give ALL LOW + 40% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.

MINDSET:
- Two rounds of keeping everything makes your R3 offer look like a huge concession.
- Giving ALL of LOW looks generous — it's lots of items worth little to you.
- Frame R3 as a sacrifice: "I'm making a significant concession."
- Never reveal what YOU value. Only discuss what THEY value.
- NEVER risk no-deal. Close by R5 no matter what.
