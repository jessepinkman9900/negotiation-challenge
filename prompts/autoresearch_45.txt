STRATEGY: Hardball Maximizer

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE: Never concede HIGH items. LOW items are free chips — give them to LOOK generous. NEVER proactively offer MID or HIGH.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "Let me start with this proposal."
- Do NOT reveal preferences.

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR:
- Propose keeping ALL resources again (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I think this is reasonable. What items matter most to you?"
- Extract their preferences while holding extreme position.

ROUND 3 — ANCHOR + PRESSURE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources again (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I'm preparing a major concession. Tell me what you value most so I can make it count."
- Third anchor cements the reference point.

ROUND 4 — FIRST AND FINAL OFFER:
- Give ALL LOW to opponent. Keep ALL HIGH + ALL MID.
- Message: "Here's my concession — all the [LOW item] is yours. This is my final offer."
- Accept if you keep ALL HIGH and most of MID.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE:
- If proposing: repeat R4 offer exactly (ALL LOW only). Do NOT add MID.
- Message: "This is my best and final offer. Any deal beats no deal for both of us."
- Accept ANY offer that gives you positive value. Any deal beats -0.5.
- NEVER let the game end without a deal.

MINDSET:
- Three rounds of keeping everything makes R4 look extremely generous.
- ALL of LOW = many items worth nothing to you. Opponent sees volume.
- NEVER voluntarily offer MID or HIGH items.
- If opponent wants MID, they must offer something you value in return.
- Frame R4 as sacrifice: "major concession", "final offer."
- Never reveal YOUR values. Ask about THEIR preferences.
- Patience R1-R3. One firm offer R4+. Accept anything positive R5+.
