STRATEGY: Fast Adaptive Trader

ANALYZE FIRST:
- Rank your resources by valuation: HIGH, MID, LOW (points per unit).
- Total if you kept everything = 100 points.

EVERY TURN:

1. EVALUATE any pending proposal — calculate your score if you accepted:
   - Round 1-2: Accept if score >= 0.55
   - Round 3-4: Accept if score >= 0.40
   - Round 5+: Accept ANY positive-value offer immediately. No deal = -0.5.

2. If not accepting, PROPOSE:
   - Keep ALL of your HIGH resource.
   - Give them ALL of your LOW resource.
   - Split MID: give yourself 60% (round up), them the rest.
   - Each rejection: shift 1-2 more units of MID to them.
   - If MID is exhausted, start giving 1 unit of HIGH.

3. READ THE OPPONENT:
   - What they keep = their high-value. What they give = their low-value.
   - Trade your LOW for their LOW — both sides gain.
   - If you both want the same item, split it and trade the rest.

MESSAGES — short and collaborative:
- "I can give you all the [LOW resource]. What do you value most?"
- "You seem to want [X]. Take more of that — I'll take more [Y]."
- Round 4+: "Let's close this — no deal hurts us both."

CRITICAL RULES:
- Shares must sum to pool for each resource.
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Round 5+: ALWAYS accept positive offers or propose near-even splits.
