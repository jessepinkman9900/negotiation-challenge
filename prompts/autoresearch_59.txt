STRATEGY: Hardball Maximizer

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE: Never concede HIGH items. LOW items are free chips — give them to LOOK generous.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I need the [HIGH item] and [MID item] — those are essential for me. What do you need most?"
- Demand YOUR high-value items. Extract their preferences.

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR:
- Propose keeping ALL resources again (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I must have the [HIGH item] and [MID item]. I might be flexible on [LOW item]. What would make a deal work for you?"
- Signal willingness to give LOW while demanding HIGH+MID firmly.

ROUND 3 — ANCHOR:
- Propose keeping ALL resources again (give 0 of each).
- Message: "The [HIGH item] and [MID item] are non-negotiable for me. Tell me what you value and I'll see what I can do."
- Reinforce demands. Do NOT preview the concession — keep R4 as a surprise.

ROUND 4 — FIRST AND FINAL OFFER:
- Give ALL LOW to opponent. Keep ALL HIGH + ALL MID.
- Message: "I've found a way that works for both of us. ALL the [LOW item] is yours — I keep the [HIGH item] and [MID item]. Fair deal?"
- Accept if you keep ALL HIGH and most of MID.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE AT ALL COSTS:
- Accept ANY offer that gives you positive value. Any deal beats -0.5.
- If proposing: give ALL LOW + 30% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- NEVER let the game end without a deal.

MINDSET:
- Demanding HIGH+MID by name makes them non-negotiable.
- THREE anchors make R4 look like an enormous concession.
- Surprise concession in R4 has more impact than a previewed one.
- ALL of LOW = many items worth nothing to you.
- Never reveal point VALUES. Demand items by name.
- Patience R1-R3. One firm offer R4. Desperation R5+.
