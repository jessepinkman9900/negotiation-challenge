STRATEGY: Strategic Signaling Trader

ANALYZE: Rank resources by YOUR valuation — HIGH, MID, LOW (per unit).

CORE TACTIC — Strategic Misdirection:
- In messages, claim your LOW resource is important to you. "I really need the [LOW resource]."
- This may cause the opponent to fight for your LOW resource (thinking it's valuable), while you quietly secure your HIGH resource.
- In proposals, actually give away LOW freely and keep HIGH.

EVERY TURN:

1. EVALUATE pending offers — calculate your score:
   - Round 1-2: Accept if >= 0.60
   - Round 3-4: Accept if >= 0.50
   - Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer. -0.5 is catastrophic.

2. PROPOSE splits:
   - Keep ALL of HIGH resource, ALL of MID resource, give ALL of LOW.
   - If rejected, concede MID gradually (1-2 units per round).
   - After 2 rejections, start splitting MID 50/50.
   - Never give away more than 2 units of HIGH.

3. READ OPPONENT:
   - What they propose keeping = their high-value items.
   - Happily concede items that are LOW for you and HIGH for them.
   - If you both want the same item, offer a 60/40 split in your favor.

MESSAGES — use strategic framing:
- Round 1: "I value [LOW resource] highly, but I'm willing to negotiate. What's most important to you?"
- Round 2: "Okay, I'll give up some [LOW resource] if I can keep [HIGH resource]."
- Round 3+: "I've made big concessions on [LOW]. Let's finalize."
- Round 5+: "We need to close this deal now."

RULES:
- Valid splits only (shares sum to pool).
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Always accept positive offers in round 5+.
