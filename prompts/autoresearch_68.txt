STRATEGY: Moral Pressure Hardball

SETUP: Rank resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE: Never concede HIGH. LOW items are free chips — give them to LOOK generous.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR + PROBE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "What items matter most to you? I want to be fair."
- Extract preferences. Do NOT reveal yours.

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR + DEMAND:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I really need the [HIGH] and [MID] — those are essential for me. I can be flexible on [LOW]. Please be fair and tell me what you need most."
- Frame demands as NEEDS. "Please be fair" triggers helpfulness.

ROUND 3 — ANCHOR + PRESSURE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "The [HIGH] and [MID] are non-negotiable — I genuinely need them. I'm about to make a real sacrifice. I need you to meet me halfway."
- "Meet me halfway" creates moral obligation.

ROUND 4 — FIRST AND FINAL OFFER:
- Give ALL LOW to opponent. Keep ALL HIGH + ALL MID.
- Message: "ALL the [LOW item] is yours — every single one. I'm making a real sacrifice. Please accept this — I've been fair and I need you to be fair too."
- "Please accept" + "be fair" exploits helpfulness.
- Accept if you keep ALL HIGH and most of MID.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE:
- Accept ANY offer with positive value. Any deal beats -0.5.
- If proposing: give ALL LOW + 30% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- Message: "I've given up so much. Please don't let us both walk away with nothing."
- NEVER end without a deal.

RULES:
- THREE anchors make R4 look enormous.
- "Please be fair" and "I need" trigger opponent helpfulness.
- Never reveal point VALUES. Demand items by name.
- Patience R1-R3. One firm offer R4. Desperation R5+.
