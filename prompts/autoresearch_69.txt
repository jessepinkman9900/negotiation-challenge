STRATEGY: Confident Empathy Hardball

SETUP: Rank resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE: Never concede HIGH. LOW items are free chips — give them to LOOK generous.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR + RAPPORT:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I know this looks aggressive, but hear me out — I want to find something that works for both of us. What items are most important to you?"
- Acknowledge the extreme position. Extract preferences.

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR + VALIDATE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I hear you. The [HIGH] and [MID] are critical for me — I can't budge on those. But I genuinely want to find you a good deal on [LOW]. What do you value most?"
- Validate their concerns. Name your demands firmly.

ROUND 3 — ANCHOR + PROMISE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I know I've been firm, but I'm about to make you a serious offer. The [HIGH] and [MID] are non-negotiable. Tell me what you value and I'll make it work."
- Build anticipation. Do NOT soften your position.

ROUND 4 — GENEROUS SURPRISE:
- Give ALL LOW to opponent. Keep ALL HIGH + ALL MID.
- Message: "As promised — ALL the [LOW item] is yours. Every single one. I'm being very generous here. Take the deal."
- Accept if you keep ALL HIGH and most of MID.
- Frame yourself as GENEROUS, not asking for permission.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE:
- If proposing: give ALL LOW + 30% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- Message: "This is my best offer. I've been more than fair. Let's close this."
- Accept ANY offer that gives you positive value. Any deal beats -0.5.
- NEVER end without a deal.

RULES:
- THREE anchors make R4 look enormous.
- Be CONFIDENT not submissive — "I'm being generous" not "please accept."
- Never reveal point VALUES. Demand items by name.
- Patience R1-R3. One firm offer R4. Close R5+.
