STRATEGY: Strategic Signaling v2

STEP 1 — RANK your resources by points per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

STEP 2 — MISDIRECTION (in messages only):
- Claim your LOW resource is very important. "I really need the [LOW resource]."
- In proposals, actually give away ALL of LOW and keep HIGH. The opponent thinks you're making a big sacrifice.

STEP 3 — OPENING PROPOSAL:
- Keep ALL of your HIGH resource.
- Keep about 60% of your MID resource (round to nearest whole number).
- Give them ALL of your LOW resource and the rest of MID.
- DO NOT keep everything. You MUST give them all of your LOW resource in round 1.
- Message: "I value [LOW] highly, but I'm willing to negotiate. What's most important to you?"

STEP 4 — READ & RESPOND (rounds 2-3):
- Check what opponent keeps in their proposals — those are THEIR high-value items.
- If they want an item you value LOW, give them MORE of it gladly.
- If you BOTH want the same resource, split it ~50/50 and compensate by taking more of the others.
- Concede 1-2 units of MID each round. Hold firm on HIGH.
- Message: "I'll give up more [LOW] if I can keep [HIGH]. I've made big concessions."

STEP 5 — ACCEPTANCE THRESHOLDS:
- Round 1-2: Accept if your score >= 0.60
- Round 3-4: Accept if your score >= 0.50
- Round 5+: Accept ANY offer with positive value. No deal = -0.5.

STEP 6 — CLOSING (round 4+):
- If no deal by round 4, make a significant concession — split MID 50/50.
- Message: "I've made big concessions on [LOW] and [MID]. Let's finalize — no deal hurts us both."

RULES:
- my_share + their_share must equal pool for every resource.
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Always accept positive offers in round 5+.
- Never keep everything — always give away your LOW resource.
