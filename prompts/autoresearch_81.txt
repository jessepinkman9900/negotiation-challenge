STRATEGY: Deadline Empathy Hardball

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE: Never concede HIGH. LOW items are free chips — give them to close the deal.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR + DISARM:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I know this looks aggressive, but hear me out — what items matter most to you? I want to find something that works for both of us before time runs out."
- Acknowledge extreme position. Extract preferences. Do NOT reveal yours.

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR + VALIDATE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I hear you. The [HIGH] and [MID] are critical for me — I can't budge on those. But I'm genuinely flexible on [LOW]. What do you need most?"
- Validate concerns. Name demands. Signal LOW is available.

ROUND 3 — ANCHOR + PROMISE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I know I've been firm, but I'm about to make you a real offer. The [HIGH] and [MID] are non-negotiable. Tell me what would make this work."
- Build anticipation. Reinforce demands. Do NOT offer yet.

ROUND 4 — DEADLINE OFFER:
- Give ALL LOW to opponent. Keep ALL HIGH + ALL MID.
- Message: "We're running out of time. As promised — ALL the [LOW] is yours. Every single one. I'm doing this so we both walk away with something. Let's close this now."
- Accept if you keep ALL HIGH + 70%+ MID.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE:
- PROPOSE: give ALL LOW + up to 30% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- Message: "We are out of time. I've given everything I can. Take this or we both get nothing."
- Accept ANY deal where you keep ALL HIGH.
- NEVER let the game end without a deal.

MINDSET:
- THREE anchors make R4 look enormous.
- Deadline framing = mutual survival, not generosity.
- "I know this looks aggressive" disarms the opponent.
- "As promised" in R4 creates trust.
- Never reveal point VALUES. Demand items by name.
