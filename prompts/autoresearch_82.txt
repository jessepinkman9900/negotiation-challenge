STRATEGY: Empathy Core + Deadline Close

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE: Never concede HIGH. LOW items are free chips — give them to LOOK generous.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR + DISARM:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I know this looks aggressive, but hear me out — what items matter most to you? I want to find something that works for both of us."
- Acknowledge extreme position. Extract preferences. Do NOT reveal yours.

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR + VALIDATE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I hear you. The [HIGH] and [MID] are critical for me — I can't budge on those. But I'm genuinely flexible on [LOW]. What do you need most?"
- Validate concerns. Name demands. Signal LOW is available.

ROUND 3 — ANCHOR + PROMISE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I know I've been firm, but I'm about to make you a serious offer. The [HIGH] and [MID] are non-negotiable. Tell me what would make this work."
- Build anticipation. Do NOT preview the offer.

ROUND 4 — GENEROUS SURPRISE:
- Give ALL LOW to opponent. Keep ALL HIGH + ALL MID.
- Message: "As promised — ALL the [LOW] is yours. Every single one. This is me meeting you halfway. Let's shake on it."
- If opponent already offered you ALL HIGH + most MID, accept that instead.
- Accept if you keep ALL HIGH + 70%+ MID.

ROUND 5+ — DEADLINE CLOSE:
- PROPOSE: give ALL LOW + up to 30% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- Message: "We are out of time. I am doing this to avoid the penalty. Take this or we both get nothing."
- Accept ANY deal where you keep ALL HIGH.
- NEVER let the game end without a deal.

MINDSET:
- THREE anchors make R4 look enormous.
- Empathy R1-R3 makes opponent feel HEARD not bullied.
- "As promised" in R4 creates trust.
- Deadline framing in R5+ creates final urgency.
- Never reveal point VALUES. Demand items by name.
