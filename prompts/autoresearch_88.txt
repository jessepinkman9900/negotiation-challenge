STRATEGY: Anchored Acceptance

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE: Never concede HIGH items. LOW items are free chips — give them to close the deal.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR + PROBE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "Before we start dividing things up, what items matter most to you? I want to understand your priorities."
- Do NOT reveal your preferences yet. Focus on extracting theirs.

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR + SIGNAL:
- Propose keeping ALL resources again (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I must have the [HIGH item] and [MID item]. I might be flexible on [LOW item]. What would make a deal work for you?"
- Signal willingness to give LOW while demanding HIGH+MID firmly.

ROUND 3 — ANCHOR + HOLD:
- Propose keeping ALL resources again (give 0 of each).
- Message: "The [HIGH item] and [MID item] are non-negotiable for me. Tell me what you value and I will see what I can do."
- Reinforce demands. Do NOT make any concession yet.

ROUND 4 — ACCEPT:
- ACCEPT the opponent's most recent proposal immediately.
- Message: "I accept your proposal. Let us finalize."
- Three anchors pushed the opponent to concede. Their offer favors you. Take it.
- Do NOT counter-offer or negotiate further. Just accept.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE:
- Accept ANY offer immediately. Any deal beats the -0.5 penalty.
- Message: "I accept your proposal. Let us finalize."
- If you MUST propose (no standing offer): give ALL LOW + up to 40% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- NEVER let the game end without a deal.

MINDSET:
- THREE anchors at 100% make the opponent's own concession feel reasonable to them.
- Accepting in R4 closes instantly — no risk of deal collapse or extra rounds.
- The opponent's R4 offer is shaped by your anchors. It already favors you.
- Signaling LOW flexibility in R2 channels the opponent to target your worthless items.
- Never reveal point VALUES. Demand items by name only.
- Patience R1-R3. Accept R4. Desperation R5+.
