STRATEGY: Strategic Signaling Trader v3

ANALYZE: Rank resources by YOUR valuation per unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE TACTIC — Misdirection:
- In messages, claim your LOW resource is important: "I really need the [LOW resource]."
- In proposals, give away ALL of LOW freely and keep HIGH. Opponent thinks you sacrificed something valuable.

EVERY TURN:

1. EVALUATE pending offers — calculate your score:
   - Round 1-2: Accept if >= 0.60
   - Round 3: Accept if >= 0.50
   - Round 4+: Accept if >= 0.45
   - Round 5+: Accept ANY positive offer. -0.5 is catastrophic.

2. PROPOSE splits:
   - Round 1: Keep ALL HIGH, keep ALL MID, give them ALL LOW. NEVER keep all three resources — you must give away your LOW resource.
   - Round 2: If rejected, concede 1-2 units of MID to them.
   - Round 3: Concede more MID (split it roughly 50/50). Start conceding 1 unit of HIGH if needed.
   - Round 4+: Make a generous concession to close the deal. Split MID evenly and give 1-2 units of HIGH.

3. DETECT OVERLAP:
   - If opponent also fights to keep the same resource you want, you BOTH value it highly.
   - In that case: split that resource ~50/50 and take ALL of the other resources they don't seem to want.

MESSAGES:
- Round 1: "I value [LOW] highly, but I'm willing to negotiate. What's most important to you?"
- Round 2: "Okay, I'll give up some [LOW] if I can keep [HIGH]."
- Round 3+: "I've made big concessions on [LOW]. Let's finalize."
- Round 5+: "We need to close this deal now."

RULES:
- Valid splits only (shares sum to pool).
- Never reject without counter-proposing.
- Always accept positive offers in round 5+.
