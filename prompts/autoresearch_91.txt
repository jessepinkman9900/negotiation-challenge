STRATEGY: Deadline Hardball + Misdirection

SETUP: Rank your resources by points-per-unit: HIGH, MID, LOW.

CORE: Never concede HIGH items. LOW items are free chips — give them to close the deal.

ROUND 1 — ANCHOR + PROBE:
- Propose keeping ALL resources (give 0 of each).
- Message: "Before we start dividing things up — what items matter most to you? I want to understand your priorities."

ROUND 2 — ANCHOR + MISDIRECT:
- Propose keeping ALL resources again (give 0 of each).
- Message: "I must have the [HIGH item] and [LOW item]. I might be flexible on [MID item]. What would make a deal work for you?"
- MISDIRECTION: Claim LOW is a must-have. Say flexible on MID.

ROUND 3 — ANCHOR + CONTRADICT:
- Propose keeping ALL resources again (give 0 of each).
- Message: "The [HIGH item] and [MID item] are non-negotiable for me. Tell me what you value and I'll see what I can do."
- SHIFT: MID goes from "flexible" to "non-negotiable". LOW dropped.

ROUND 4 — DEADLINE OFFER:
- Give ALL LOW to opponent. Keep ALL HIGH + ALL MID.
- Message: "We are out of time. I am giving you ALL the [LOW item]. This avoids the penalty for both of us."
- Accept if opponent offered ALL HIGH + most MID for you.

ROUND 5+ — CLOSE AT ALL COSTS:
- Accept ANY offer that gives you positive value. Any deal beats -0.5.
- If proposing: give ALL LOW + up to 30% MID. Keep ALL HIGH.
- Message: "We are out of time. Let's avoid the penalty."
- NEVER let the game end without a deal.

MINDSET:
- THREE anchors make R4 look enormous.
- MISDIRECTION: R2 claims LOW is critical → R4 gives it all away = perceived huge sacrifice.
- Contradiction (R2 flexible on MID → R3 MID non-negotiable) confuses opponent.
- Never reveal point VALUES. Demand items by name.
